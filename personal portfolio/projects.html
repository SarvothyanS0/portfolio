Project 1

AI Image Generator

The webpage shown is a locally hosted AI Image Generator interface designed with a vibrant and modern aesthetic. At the top, there's a bold purple header containing the title "AI Image Generator" on the left and a navigation menu on the right with links labeled Home, Info, Contact, and a prominent Log in button styled in black with a user icon. Below the header, the main content is centered within an orange gradient background. The main heading "Your Ai Image Generator" is displayed in bright yellow text, followed by a short description that explains how users can input creative prompts to generate AI images. It also provides example prompt phrases such as "Best Quality, Masterpiece, Extremely Detailed, 4K," and more to inspire users. The central part of the page showcases three sample images: a red F1 car labeled “F1 car racing on the track,” a cat in an astronaut suit labeled “Cat wearing spacesuit,” and an abstract digital artwork labeled “Beauty woven into every pixel.” These images demonstrate the kind of content users might generate using the tool. Beneath the images is an input field where users can type their custom prompt, along with a black Generate button to initiate image generation. The overall layout is clean and user-friendly, with rounded card containers, consistent spacing, and a bright color palette that emphasizes creativity and engagement.

Project 2

Tribute page

This tribute webpage is dedicated to Satya Nadella, the CEO of Microsoft, celebrating his leadership, values, and accomplishments. The header prominently displays his name with a bright gradient background, accompanied by a photo of Nadella in front of the Microsoft logo and a powerful quote: “Our industry does not respect tradition — it only respects innovation.” The design is colorful and dynamic, symbolizing Nadella’s innovative influence.
The "About Satya Nadella" section provides an overview of his journey, highlighting how he transformed Microsoft since becoming CEO in 2014. It emphasizes his focus on empathy, collaboration, and innovation, which revitalized the company’s culture. The "Admirable Qualities" section outlines his key traits—Empathy, Vision, Humility, and Innovation—explaining how these qualities shaped Microsoft's growth. The content is arranged in neat cards with colorful backgrounds and shadows, enhancing readability.
Further down, the "Inspiration" section details his journey from Hyderabad to Redmond, portraying his life as an inspiring story of leadership, curiosity, and purpose. The page ends with a motivational quote and a footer linking to more information, signed with “Made with ♥ by Sarvothyan S.” The overall layout is visually engaging and informative, honoring Nadella’s legacy with creativity and respect.

Project 3

Quiz app

This quiz application is built using Flask and features an engaging web interface for users to test their knowledge across different difficulty levels—Easy, Medium, and Hard. The homepage presents a striking black background with an orange circular design, displaying a silhouette of a character. Users select their preferred difficulty level and proceed to answer multiple-choice questions.
The quiz interface features a countdown timer and includes basic questions covering math, geography, science, and general knowledge. Players are challenged with queries like “What is 2 + 2?”, “What is the capital of France?”, and “Which planet is known as the Red Planet?”. The visual elements include a dark theme with colorful splashes, accompanied by an illustrated character.
After completing the quiz, users receive their results on a summary page, showing their score along with correct and incorrect answers. The results interface adopts a blue-green gradient background, adding a professional touch. The application provides an excellent way to evaluate knowledge, learn from mistakes, and track improvement.
